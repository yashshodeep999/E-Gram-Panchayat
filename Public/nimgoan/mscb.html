About Mahavitaran (MSEDCL)

Electricity Dept

Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL), commonly known as Mahavitaran, is responsible for electricity distribution across Maharashtra. It provides power supply, maintenance, billing, and customer support services to domestic, agricultural, and commercial consumers.

The Mahavitaran office supports power supply, maintenance of meters/transformers/lines, billing, and complaint redressal.

Roles & Responsibilities

Public Service
  • Electricity distribution for homes, agriculture, and businesses
  • Maintenance of power lines, transformers, poles, and meters
  • Electricity bill generation and payment management
  • Resolution of power outage/low voltage/fault complaints
  • New connections and load increase/decrease (as per rules)
  • Ensuring safe and reliable power supply

Available Services

Customer Service

Electricity Supply

  • Domestic electricity connection
  • Agricultural power supply
  • Commercial/Industrial connection
  • Temporary connection for events – subject to availability

Customer Support

  • Bill complaints/corrections
  • Meter inspection/replacement (as per rules)
  • Name change / Load change requests
  • Power outage complaint registration & tracking

Citizen Guidance

User Friendly
  • Carry your Consumer Number and bill/receipt when visiting the office
  • If power is off, first check the main switch/MCB; then register a complaint
  • Pay bills on time; delay may lead to late fee or disconnection
  • Report fallen wires, leaning poles, or sparking immediately

Electrical Safety Instructions

Very Important
  • Do NOT touch broken or fallen electric wires
  • Maintain a safe distance from transformers/substations
  • Do not operate switches/boards/plugs with wet hands
  • Use only ISI-certified electrical equipment
