ATM / Customer Service Point (CSP)

Quick Banking

ATM/CSP helps villagers for quick banking and government payments without going far.

Available Services

  • Cash withdrawal facility
  • Balance enquiry & mini statement
  • DBT / pension withdrawals
  • Basic digital payment help (UPI/QR)

ATM Safety Rules

  • Cover keypad while entering PIN
  • Do not accept help from strangers
  • Avoid sharing OTP / PIN / card details
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
If ATM fails: Keep the receipt (if any) and contact your bank branch/call center for complaint.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to open a bank account?

Carry Aadhaar, PAN (if available), photo and mobile number. Visit the branch/CSP and complete KYC.

What is DBT and why is it important?

DBT means Direct Benefit Transfer. Government benefits come directly into your bank account.

What to do if I receive a fraud call?

Do not share OTP/PIN. End the call and contact your bank branch/call center immediately.
