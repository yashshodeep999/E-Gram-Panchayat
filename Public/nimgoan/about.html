Welcome to Nimgoan Ketki Gram Panchayat

The Nimgoan Ketki Gram Panchayat is committed to the development and well-being of our village. As a governing body, we focus on improving the infrastructure, public services, and overall quality of life for our villagers.

Our Vision

We envision Nimgoan Ketki as a self-sustained, developed, and inclusive village where the needs of every citizen are met with transparency, accountability, and efficiency. We strive to promote agricultural advancements, education, healthcare, and rural infrastructure while preserving our rich cultural heritage.

What We Do

  • Promote sustainable agricultural practices
  • Improve rural infrastructure and roads
  • Support water conservation efforts
  • Enhance health and sanitation services
  • Organize cultural and community events
  • Implement housing and welfare schemes

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve our community with dedication and transparency. We work closely with the people of Nimgoan Ketki to ensure sustainable development and social welfare, fostering a sense of unity and progress.

Meet Our Leaders

Our executive board is made up of dedicated individuals who work tirelessly to bring positive change and development to Nimgoan Ketki. Each member contributes their expertise to various initiatives that shape the future of our village.
