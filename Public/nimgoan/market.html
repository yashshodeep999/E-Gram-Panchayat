Leaf Market (पान बाजार) – Every Tuesday

The Leaf Market in Nimgoan Ketki, locally known as the पान बाजार, is held every Tuesday. It is dedicated to the wholesale and retail trade of betel leaves (paan), attracting traders and vendors from surrounding villages.

Day Tuesday
Location Near Gram Panchayat Office (Update exact spot)
  • Specialized market for fresh betel leaves
  • Supplies to local and regional vendors
  • Popular among farmers and traders dealing in cash crops
  • Wholesale + retail buying options

Weekly Market (साप्ताहिक बाजार) – Every Saturday

The Weekly Market is a large community gathering held every Saturday. It offers a wide variety of goods ranging from farm produce to daily essentials, clothing, kitchenware, and more.

Day Saturday
Location Near Gram Panchayat Office (Update exact spot)
  • Fresh vegetables, fruits, cereals, and household items
  • Street food stalls (as per availability)
  • Operates from morning till sunset
  • Good place for daily shopping at one place
