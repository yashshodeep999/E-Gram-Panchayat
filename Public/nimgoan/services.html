Our Services

The Nimgoan Ketki Gram Panchayat offers a variety of services to enhance the quality of life in our village. Below are some of the key services provided:

Healthcare

Providing access to quality healthcare services including free health check-ups, vaccination drives, and health awareness programs.

Water Conservation

Initiatives to save water through efficient irrigation methods and community-led water management projects.

Education

Supporting educational development by improving local schools, providing scholarships, and offering skill development programs.

Agriculture Support

Promoting sustainable agricultural practices by providing training, financial aid, and access to modern farming tools.

Sanitation

Improving sanitation facilities in the village to ensure a clean and healthy environment for all residents.

Rural Infrastructure

Developing and maintaining roads, bridges, and public buildings to improve connectivity and the overall infrastructure of the village.
